Daily flows for all levels. Set duration of session and customize moves. Choose to include modifications if needed to facilitate your best flow.
POWER FLOWS
Intense sessions that make you break a sweat every time. Set duration of workout and customize moves. Specify what area of the body you want to focus on; turn modifications on if needed.

Lighter sessions that focus more on the mental state during the flow. Set duration of workout and length of time to hold each pose with a minimum of 60 seconds for each pose. Include modifications if needed.
